Top Priority
📈

Business & Economic Growth

Newark has enormous untapped economic potential. I'll work to attract new businesses, support our existing local shops and entrepreneurs, and create good-paying jobs — right here in our city, for our residents.

  • Cut red tape that slows small business permits and startups
  • Attract new commercial development to vacant properties
  • Partner with Fremont and regional employers to bring jobs to Newark
  • Support a thriving downtown that residents are proud of
  • Invest in workforce development programs for Newark residents
